## *Boolean Algebra Cheat Sheet* 

**Annulment**   
A+1=1  
A*0=0 

**Identity**  
A+0=A  
A*1=A 

**Idempotent**  
A+A=A  
A*A=A 

**Absorptive**  
A+(A\*B)=A  
A*(A+B)=A 

**Associative**  
(A+B)+C=A+(B+C)  
(A\*B)\*C=A\*(B*C) 

**Commutative**   
A+B=B+A  
A\*B=B*A 

**Distributive**   
A+(B\*C)=(A+B)*(A+C)  
A\*(B+C)=(A\*B)+(A\*C) 

**Morgan's Laws**   
(A+B)'=A'\*B'  
(A*B)'=A'+B'
